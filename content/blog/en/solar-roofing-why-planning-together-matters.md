When roofing and solar are treated as separate conversations, homeowners often face duplicate labor, delayed permits, or the risk of reinstall costs later. Early coordination reduces all of that.

A roof assessment before solar design helps determine whether installation should move forward immediately or whether roofing work should happen first.

## Benefits of coordinated planning

- Avoids panel removal and reinstallation costs later
- Creates a cleaner sequence for scheduling and permits
- Improves long-term confidence in the full upgrade

## Why this matters financially

The cheapest quote on paper can become more expensive when roof condition is ignored. Planning both scopes together helps protect the installation, the schedule, and the long-term value of the project.
